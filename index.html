LeetViz

Interactive problem visualizations directly inside LeetCode.

LeetViz seamlessly integrates into LeetCode to help you visually understand what complex problems are actually asking. When you open a supported LeetCode problem, LeetViz automatically loads an interactive, step-by-step animation in your Chrome side panel — without obstructing your coding environment.

Key Features

  • Problem Dry-Runs: Watch a frame-by-frame visualization of the exact problem statement and example test cases. Understand what the problem is asking before you start coding, without the solution being spoiled!
  • Progressive Hints: Unlock gentle structural hints when you're stuck, rather than immediately jumping to the discussion tabs.
  • Custom Test Cases: Run the visualizer against LeetCode's exact preset examples.
  • Community-Driven: All visualizers are open-source. Don't see a visualizer you need? Generate one with AI and submit it to the growing LeetViz registry!
